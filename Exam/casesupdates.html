Philippines


Last updated: Thu Oct 08 2020




All 331,869


This is the total number of confirmed COVID-19 cases in the Philippines. Some of the patients in this number are active, recovered, or dead.

Active 51,482


These are patients with confirmed COVID-19 via RT-PCR of specimens collected from the nose or throat. Active cases are defined as those who have not yet recovered or not yet died from their illness.

Died 6,069



These are patients with confirmed COVID-19 cases who have died.

Recovered 274,318

These are patients who were confirmed to have COVID-19 via RT-PCR of specimens collected from the nose or throat. Recovered patients are those who have no or minimal symptoms and have either tested negative when a repeat RT-PCR of nasal or throat swabs was performed or completed a 14-day quarantine period.

Asymptomatic 5,059

Patients with but for whom SARS-CoV-2 RNA was detected from specimens collected from the nose or throat using RT-PCR. These patients can still spread the infection to other people.

Quarantined 63,243


These are COVID-19 patients who are quarantined
